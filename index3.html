KG College of Arts and Science (KGCAS), located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, was established in 2005 by KG Information Systems (KGiSL). The college offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and integrated programs, with a focus on providing industry-oriented education, particularly in IT and ITES. KGCAS is ISO 9001:2008 certified and accredited by NAAC.
